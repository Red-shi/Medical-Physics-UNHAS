Our Areas of Focus

Dedicating the science of physics to advance healthcare services.

Radiotherapy

The application of physics in the planning and delivery of radiation for precise and safe cancer therapy.

Radiodiagnostics

Optimization and quality assurance in medical imaging modalities such as CT Scan, MRI, and X-ray.

Nuclear Medicine

Utilization of radioisotopes for functional body diagnosis and internal therapy (theranostics).
